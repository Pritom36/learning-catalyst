Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is the Flashcard Learning App?

The Flashcard Learning App is an educational tool designed to help users learn and practice vocabulary through interactive flashcards.

How does it work?

Create your own flashcards or use pre-made sets. Study them through various learning modes and track your progress over time.

Technical Questions

What devices are supported?

Our app works on all modern devices including smartphones, tablets, and computers through any web browser.
